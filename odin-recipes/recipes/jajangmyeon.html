Jajangmyeon

Home

Description

Jajangmyeon is a Korean dish made with a simple batter of flour and water, pan-fried to create a crispy, savory pancake. It's often enjoyed as a snack or side dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Chopped green onions (optional)

    • Steps

    1. In a bowl, mix flour, water, and salt to form a batter.
    2. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
    3. Pour a ladle of batter into the pan, spreading it thinly.
    4. Cook until golden brown on both sides.
    5. Serve hot with dipping sauce or toppings of choice.
